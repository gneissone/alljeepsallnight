Tournament Info

Hey there!
Do you miss the days of Swollen Toe and Lungbuster when we would meet in the mountains to cool off and play each other at altitude? Well do we have the tournament for you! We're organizing a 1-day, unsanctioned, mixed tourney in picturesque Fraser, CO called Thumber Camp. We'll gather at the YMCA of the Rockies (Snow Mountain Ranch) Friday, Aug. 9 for a bonfire, smores, and bug juice. Saturday there'll be ultimate during the day (Fraser Valley Rec) and a party in town at night. Did we tell you that it's only 1 day of ultimate!? That leaves Sunday for you and your team to enjoy the area. We'll have camp counselors leading mountain biking, hiking, and arts and crafts. In addition, the YMCA has mini golf course, indoor pool, tennis courts, reservoir fishing, lawn games. So much fun you'll be writing home to your parents about it!

Yours now and always,
Thumber Camp tournament committee

Team registration is now closed. Individual team members can register and pay here. Captains meeting at 9:45 on Saturday, games start at 10.

Location

The tournament will be held at the Fraser Valley Sports complex in the beautiful Colorado highcountry, approximately 1.5 hours west of Denver. Set course for 220 County Rd 522, Fraser, CO 80442.

Dogs are allowed at the fields but they must remain on leash. Please, no glass. Snacks and water will be provided.

Lodging

Housing is available at Snow Mountain Ranch. Come stay with us and join in the fun! There are cabins (~10 people), rooms (~4-5 people) and camping options available. Prices include meals. Cost and availability is difficult to determine on The Snow Mountain Ranch website, it's best just to call them at 888-613-9622.

Tournament Party

Using our super powerful party negotiating skills, we have arranged a super fun party at Ullrs Tavern near Fraser. There shall be drinks. Bella's Bartok out of Northampton, MA happens to be in town playing a raucous set of circus punk.



Ullrs is a 5 minute drive from Fraser and 15 minutes from the YMCA. Please plan ahead! Do not count on rideshares.

Sunday activities

After a night nestled in our bunks, Jeep counselors will lead interested campers in some fun (informal) activities on Sunday. You can opt to join the famed explorer of tall things BenG on a hike, speed off for a mountain bike outing with Rodeo (bring your own bike), join yoga-master Julia and the arts & crafts crew for a relaxing morning, or trot off into the sunset/late morning sunrise-ish thing on horseback with Addi. Registrants, please fill out the interest form sent via email to indicate interest.

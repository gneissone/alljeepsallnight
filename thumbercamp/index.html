Tournament Info

Hey there!
Do you miss the days of Swollen Toe and Lungbuster when we would meet in the mountains to cool off and play each other at altitude? Well do we have the tournament for you! We're organizing a 1-day, unsanctioned, mixed tourney in picturesque Steamboat Springs, CO called Thumber Camp. Saturday there'll be ultimate during the day and a party in town at night. Did we tell you that it's only 1 day of ultimate!? That leaves Sunday for you and your team to enjoy the area. There are plenty of opportunities for mountain biking, hiking, and of course, arts and crafts. So much fun you'll be writing home to your parents about it!

Yours now and always,
Thumber Camp tournament committee

Team registration for 2024 is coming soon. Reach out to the tournament coordinators at thumbercamp@gmail.com with questions.

Schedule and format

We're still working out these details. In the past, each team has played 4 games and had 1 bye.
Games will begin in the mid-morning and last until the late afternoon.

Games have been followed by pizza at the fields in the past! Stay tuned.
Evening - Party at a location in town.

Location

The tournament will be held in Steamboat Springs in the beautiful Colorado highcountry, less than 3 hours west of Denver. Set course for Yampa River Valley, stay tuned for field specifics.

Please, no glass at the fields. Snacks and water will be provided. Dog policy TBD.

Lodging

The nearest Forest Service campgrounds are Dry Lake and Meadows. Reservations are available, as are a handful of first-come, first serve sites. Dispersed camping may be available along FS Road 60 near Buffalo Pass, but you may want to check with the Forest Service Office at (970)-723-2700 or (970)-870-2299.

Tournament Party

There shall be drinks. Note, please do not approach any poles you may encounter.

Please plan ahead for rides! If you're staying near town in Steamboat, you may be able to hop on the bus to get to the party. Do not count on rideshares.

Sunday activities

After a night nestled in our bunks, join us for some fun (informal) activities on Sunday. We will discuss plans following games. Past activities have included hiking, mountain biking, yoga, and arts & crafts.

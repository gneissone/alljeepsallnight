Tournament Info

Hey there!
Do you miss the days of Swollen Toe and Lungbuster when we would meet in the mountains to cool off and play each other at altitude? Well do we have the tournament for you! We're organizing a 1-day, unsanctioned, mixed tourney in picturesque Fraser, CO called Thumber Camp. We'll gather at the YMCA of the Rockies (Snow Mountain Ranch) Friday, Aug. 9 for a bonfire, smores, and bug juice. Saturday there'll be ultimate during the day (Fraser Valley Rec) and a party in town at night. Did we tell you that it's only 1 day of ultimate!? That leaves Sunday for you and your team to enjoy the area. We'll have camp counselors leading mountain biking, hiking, and arts and crafts. In addition, the YMCA has mini golf course, indoor pool, tennis courts, reservoir fishing, lawn games. So much fun you'll be writing home to your parents about it!

We'll have registration up soon. Cost is expected to be ~$30/person. For now, if you're interested in putting a team together and would like to be updated periodically simply email us and let us know. Thanks!

Yours now and always,
Thumber Camp tournament committee

Location

The tournament will be held at the Fraser Valley Sports complex in the beautiful Colorado highcountry, approximately 1.5 hours west of Denver. Set course for 220 County Rd 522, Fraser, CO 80442.

Lodging

Housing is available at Snow Mountain Ranch. Come stay with us and join in the fun! There are cabins (~10 people), rooms (~4-5 people) and camping options available. Don't be fooled by the prices, they include meals.

Tournament Party

We are currently using our super powerful party negotiating skills to arrange a super fun party at a local Fraser establishment. Stay tuned.

Tournament Info

Hey there!
Do you miss the days of Swollen Toe and Lungbuster when we would meet in the mountains to cool off and play each other at altitude? Well do we have the tournament for you! We're organizing a 1-day, unsanctioned, mixed tourney in picturesque Fraser, CO called Thumber Camp. Saturday there'll be ultimate during the day (Fraser Valley Rec) and a party in town at night. Did we tell you that it's only 1 day of ultimate!? That leaves Sunday for you and your team to enjoy the area. There are plenty of opportunities for mountain biking, hiking, and of course, arts and crafts. So much fun you'll be writing home to your parents about it!

Yours now and always,
Thumber Camp tournament committee

Team registration for 2022 is by invite only. Cost is $20/person. Reach out to the tournament coordinators at thumbercamp@gmail.com with questions.

Schedule and format

Each team will play 4 games and have 1 bye.
Games begin at 10am, followed by rounds at 11:30, 1, 2:30, and 4.

5:30pm - Pizza at the fields
Evening - Party at Fisher's Bar

Location

The tournament will be held at the Fraser Valley Sports complex in the beautiful Colorado highcountry, approximately 1.5 hours west of Denver. Set course for 220 County Rd 522, Fraser, CO 80442.

Dogs are allowed at the fields but they must remain on leash. Please, no glass. Snacks and water will be provided.

Lodging

The nearest Forest Service campgrounds are St. Louis Creek and Idlewild. Both are first-come, first-served. Dispersed camping may be available along FS Road 128 and Road 72, but you may want to check with the Forest Service Office at 970-295-6600 or VisitARP@usda.gov.

Additional lodging is available at Snow Mountain Ranch. Visit their site or call 888-613-9622 for more information.

Tournament Party

This year we will gather at Fisher's Bar in Fraser [Google Map]. There shall be drinks. Note, please do not approach any poles you may encounter.

Please plan ahead for rides! If you're staying near town in Fraser or Winter Park, you may be able to hop on The Lift Black Line to get home from the party. Do not count on rideshares.

Sunday activities

After a night nestled in our bunks, join us for some fun (informal) activities on Sunday. We will discuss plans following games. Past activities have included hiking, mountain biking, yoga, and arts & crafts.
